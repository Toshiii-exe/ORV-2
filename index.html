Overview

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint (ORV) is a South Korean web novel penned by Sing-Shong, captivating readers with its unique narrative style and intricate plot. The story follows Kim Dok-ja, a mundane office worker whose life changes dramatically when he finds himself within the world of a web novel he was once a devoted reader of. With an intimate knowledge of the novel’s plot, Kim Dok-ja transitions from an observer to a central player, maneuvering through challenges with his newfound abilities.

The novel masterfully combines elements of fantasy, action, and psychological depth, exploring profound themes such as fate, free will, and the nature of storytelling. The dynamic narrative features unexpected twists and an evolving storyline that keeps readers on the edge of their seats. ORV is acclaimed for its engaging plot, multifaceted characters, and its exploration of the boundaries between fiction and reality.

The world of ORV is rich with complex characters, each with their own motivations and backstories, contributing to a narrative that is as thought-provoking as it is entertaining. Whether you're a fan of epic fantasy, intricate plots, or character-driven stories, Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint offers a compelling and immersive experience.

Main Characters

Discover the key figures who bring the world of Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint to life:

Kim Dok-ja

The protagonist, Kim Dok-ja, is an ordinary office worker who gains extraordinary powers when he finds himself within the novel he was reading. His deep understanding of the story’s mechanics and events grants him a unique advantage as he navigates the novel’s complex world and seeks to reshape his destiny.

Yoo Jong-hyuk

A significant character whose initial antagonistic stance evolves into a crucial alliance. Yoo Jong-hyuk is a powerful and enigmatic figure whose personal goals and past significantly impact the story’s progression.

Han Sooyoung

Han Sooyoung is a character with a mysterious background whose evolving relationship with Kim Dok-ja is central to the story’s development. Her insights and actions play a pivotal role in navigating the challenges faced by the characters.

Lee Gilyoung

Lee Gilyoung, a younger but integral character, provides essential support and perspective to Kim Dok-ja. His role in the narrative highlights the importance of teamwork and personal growth within the story.
